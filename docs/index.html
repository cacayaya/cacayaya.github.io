About Me

I’m a Fourth year Ph.D. student at Purdue University under the supervision of Prof. Ananth Grama. I also work with Prof. Zhangyang Wang and Prof. Junyuan Hong. Prior to my PhD, I completed my undergraduate studies in Electronic Information Engineering with a minor in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Science and Technology of China, where I fostered my curiosity and passion for research.

Research interests

  • LLM post-training, particularly on synthetic data generation, reward modeling and self-improvement.
  • LLM training and inference efficiency
  • Trustworthy ML
